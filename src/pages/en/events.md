## Twenty years of SUDIN — a dinner in support of the Association

In 2006, twenty years ago, the SUDIN association was founded and the Ireneo Dud Vocational Training Center was established. We will celebrate this important anniversary together during our traditional dinner in support of the Association.

- **When:** Friday 12 June 2026, 8:00 pm
- **Where:** Azienda Agricola Petrucco, Buttrio (UD), Italy
